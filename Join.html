Interested in joining the lab?

The job market in science and biomedical fields is becoming increasingly competitive and engaging in research is a great way to enhance your competitiveness. I have high expectations of grad- and under-graduate researchers and will mentor students to help them achieve their career goals.


There are many reasons to do research, including (1) enhancing your resume, (2) developing advanced techniques, (3) learning the scientific method, (4) contributing to science, and (5) impacting the world.


If you are interesting in joining my lab, email me at kgustafson@astate.edu and include (1) your resume, and (2) a statement about why you want to join my lab and (3) how my lab would help you achieve your career goals.
